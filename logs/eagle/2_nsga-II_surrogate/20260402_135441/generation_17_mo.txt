A good move is one that sets up attack range, defense coverage, building access, or efficient harvesting on the following turn.
Attack commitment:
If combat units already exist and no immediate home defense is required, prefer pressure on the enemy over passive waiting.
Once an offensive move has started, continue the attack unless the battlefield clearly turns unfavorable.
Do not stop outside the enemy base area just to wait for a slightly larger force.
Early game strategy:
Establish a stable economy by producing workers and harvesting resources.
Build a barracks as soon as resources allow to enable military production.
Avoid unnecessary aggression before the economy is set up unless the enemy is exposed.
