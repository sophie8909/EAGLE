Offensive units should prioritize engaging the enemy by moving towards their positions or key infrastructure rather than pausing in open areas.
ANTI-STALL RULES:
Do not leave workers idle if they can harvest, build, defend, or attack productively.
Do not leave bases or barracks idle when resources are sufficient for useful production.
Do not keep combat units near the base without a clear defensive reason.
TIME-BASED STRATEGY:
Early game (turns 0-100): Focus on harvesting and building a barracks.
Mid game (turns 101-200): Shift toward producing combat units and applying pressure if the enemy is vulnerable.
Late game (turns 201+): Prioritize aggressive attacks on the enemy base and production structures to secure victory.
